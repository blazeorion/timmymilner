COMPOSER

As a film music composer, I seek to enhance underlying emotions and drive that already exist in the film, especially in the mind of the director and writer, as well as bring a new aspect to the aural experience of the film. Music is something that has, since the beginning, been a tool to voice the unspeakable things of the human soul. As such, the right music at the right time in film and media does not simply add some gimmick that an audience might expect, but it connects the viewer to the characters and world of the film in a whole new dimension. Using state of the art technology as well as art music forms and theory, I seek to combine whatever sounds necessary in a tasteful manner to achieve the desired effect on the listener. My goal as a film music composer is to become apart of the story telling in order to enhance the final product, it is not to pull the spot light onto myself as some composers tend to do. If you are a director, writer, or producer who is interested in working with me on a project, I would love to take the time to chat.

Songwriter

Songwriting is something I dreamed of doing since as young as five years old when Elvis Presley and the Beach Boys were my biggest inspirations. I wrote my first real song when I was eleven years old and continued to refine my craft and gain dimension lyrically and musically. Through my studying contemporary art music composition at Chapman University as well as studies in literature and poetry I have made a serious dedication to the art of writing song. The guitar as well as the mandolin are as close to my song writing as my vocal chords are. I hope to be recording albums soon to feature a select few of many songs I’ve written.

WORSHIP LEADER

Much of what I write in my songs, are naturally completely connected to my spirituality. I believe in one true God who created this earth we live in and all that is within it; that He sent His son Jesus Christ to carry all the sin, shame and death of the world of yesterday, today, and tomorrow in order to bring life and freedom to all of humanity who believe and trust in Him. I feel I’ve been gifted with music to bring Glory to the Creator from whom it came. I don’t seek however, to condemn anybody through it, but rather to inspire the Love that I have received by my Heavenly Father. It is good stuff. I seek to worship God in all I do. The songs I write are most often love songs to God, songs that express my understanding of the human experience, and sometimes just songs I write for plain fun. As a worship leader in a Church setting, I hope to humbly share revelation and lead people of the Church into the tangible presence of the Holy Spirit and the showers of God’s love through music. Because music is so intertwined with the Human soul it tends to be a way that God reaches his Children in power. My hope is that God would use me in Faith to move mountains and stumbling blocks that keep the Church abroad from receiving His love, by bringing people together in simple songs of love to the One who started everything.

Instructor

Teaching to others what I have had the blessing of learning over many years is a new blessing in and of itself. From slowly learning Jimi Hendrix at age 11 to leading various rock/pop bands, to playing Bach on the classical guitar while a music student at Chapman University, I know what it feels like to not know a skill but to earnestly seek to master one. I was never a prodigy but rather acquired talent through years of practice and dedication, so teaching comes natural to me; because I know how it feels to not know, but to want to. I can teach beginning to advanced in most styles of both acoustic and electric guitar. I incorporate music theory into every lesson and can teach as much theory as the student is interested in (this would be especially useful for those who are looking to be song-writers). I also teach beginning to intermediate electric bass. I can teach beginning piano. I also have some formal voice training and can give helpful tips in that area. Basically, the lesson is tailored custom to each student and their specific needs/wants. I’ve taught young children of age 9 as well as adults of age 23. I’m willing and excited to teach any age from five to 99. Its never too late, and its never too early. I can provide character references, as well as skill references if needed. I have an open door policy when teaching from my apartment; I require the minor’s parent to be present but will make you feel welcome (wifi and coffee available). I live on the border of Newport Beach and Costa Mesa and am willing to drive to your house for the lesson for a $5 additional charge if you live within 15 miles of me. If you or someone you know are hoping to take lessons with me I would love to talk to you! You can find my contact information here.
